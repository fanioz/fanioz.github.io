This page is all about the people that I learned so much from, or those that (indirectly) contributed to this site.

Robert C. Martin (Uncle Bob)

Everyone in our industry knows this guy. He is best known for his books Clean Code, The Clean Coder and Clean Architecture. Furthermore he is one of the founders of the Agile Manifesto and the Manifesto for Software Craftsmanship.

Martin Fowler

Martin Fowler is an author and speaker of many books in our industry. He is dedicated to improving the profession of software development.
