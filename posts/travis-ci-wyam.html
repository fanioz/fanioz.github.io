Travis CI setup for Wyam

Tags: C# , Continuous Integration , GitHub , Wyam

To publish your Wyam site on GitHub Pages it can be useful to use Travis CI to do your builds fully automatically.

First of all, you should create an account at travis-ci.com and link your GitHub account.
